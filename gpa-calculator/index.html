Frequently asked questions

Why are courses weighted by credits?

A 4-credit course represents more coursework than a 1-credit course, so it should count more toward your overall average — credit-weighting reflects that a grade in a bigger class carries more weight than one in a smaller class.

Does every school use this exact same scale?

Most US schools use something close to this standard 4.0 scale, but exact grade-point values (especially for +/- grades) vary by institution — check your school's official scale if you need an exact match.

Is this my cumulative GPA or semester GPA?

Whichever you enter courses for — list just this semester's courses for a semester GPA, or your entire transcript for a cumulative GPA.
